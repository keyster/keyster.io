Keyster
A password manager with tact
Modern Software
Keyster will always be completely free and open source. Any password manager that offers less than that should never be used, considering the importance of the data at hand. Another major goal of Keyster is usability — our UI is clean and consistent across all platforms. We also offer a web client for quick use on unauthorized computers or devices that we don't currently support.
Device Sync and Local Backup
Any changes you make to your account on one device are automatically synced to the rest. All applications store backups locally, in the event that either you or our servers are offline.
Uncompromising Security
As a password manager, Keyster's primary goal is to keep your accounts safe. We will never send or store information that could reveal your master key or any generated passwords. Because of this, Keyster cannot offer services such as autofill, and you cannot generate passwords without entering your master key. While convenient, these practices make your passwords vulnerable to anyone with access to your device.

The end result is that an attacker will not be able to extract any meaningful data about your password from our servers, your computer, or the data sent between them. If you are interested in the benefits of password managers and Keyster specifically, we invite you to read our Crypto page.
